# Fu He Hui (福和慧)

## What this work actually does
Modern Chinese vegetarian tasting-menu restaurant — Michelin two-star (2026), Asia 50 Best #19 (2024). The only fine-dining vegetarian in the Shanghai two-star tier.

## Technical mechanism
Tony Lu (group culinary director, Fu Group) treats vegetables with protein-level seriousness. Set seasonal tasting menu in a converted villa on 愚园路.

## Why it matters for the topic's stated goals
Solves a recurring problem: where to take a vegetarian guest where the default "best in Shanghai" lists fail. Also makes a good unexpected-impression choice for omnivores.

## What is reusable
The 'serious vegetarian fine dining' positioning is rare in Shanghai; Fu He Hui is the dominant reference point.

## What is not safely transferable
Not casual. Tasting-menu format means it's a 2-3 hour commitment, not a drop-in lunch.

## Evidence quality
High — Michelin two-star + Asia 50 Best + multi-year stability + part of the well-documented Fu Group portfolio.
