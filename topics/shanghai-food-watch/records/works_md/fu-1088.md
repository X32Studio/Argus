# Fu 1088 (福一零八八)

## What this work actually does
Flagship of the Fu Group villa-restaurant series — refined Shanghainese in a 1930s three-story villa, all dining in private rooms.

## Technical mechanism
Private-room-only model in a heritage villa, butler-style service, classical 本帮/江浙 menu. Michelin one-star (2026 — kept).

## Why it matters for the topic's stated goals
Probably the single best business-dinner default in Shanghai in the 600-900¥/person tier. The room is the experience.

## What is reusable
The model has been extended to Fu 1015 and Fu 1039 (siblings) and to Fu He Hui (the vegetarian variant). Group-lineage edge connects all four.

## What is not safely transferable
Set up for slow, leisurely, no-music dinners. Not for quick lunches or casual drop-ins.

## Evidence quality
Medium — strong editorial coverage and Michelin retention, but reviews repeat the "elegant villa" framing without deeper distinguishers between Fu 1088 vs siblings.
