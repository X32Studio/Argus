# 遇外滩 (新天地店) Yu Waitan (Xintiandi)

## 1. What this work actually does
Contemporary 闽菜 (Min / Fujian cuisine) fine dining in 新天地, distinguished by a parallel tea-pairing track served from a dedicated 茶寮 lounge area. Promoted from Black Pearl 二钻 (2025) to 三钻 (2026) in one year.

## 2. Technical mechanism
Tasting-menu format with 餐茶融合 (food-and-tea fusion) sets as a structured upsell — not loose tea service, a treated-as-tasting-track parallel. Head chef Chen Zhiping (陈志评) won the 2026 Black Pearl 年轻主厨奖 (Young Chef Award).

## 3. Why it matters for the topic's stated goals
Min cuisine has been the missing wedge in Shanghai's fine-dining ontology — neither 本帮 / 江浙 nor 粤 / 川. Yu Waitan is the new canonical reference for 闽菜 in Shanghai. Promotion velocity (2钻 → 3钻 in one year) is itself a strong signal.

## 4. What is reusable
- 餐茶融合 as a tasting-track parallel (rather than incidental tea service) — a format other tasting-menu Chinese restaurants haven't tried.
- New regional-cuisine wedges are competitively open in Shanghai 中餐 fine dining.

## 5. What is not safely transferable
Per-person ~941¥ + reservation-driven format = not relevant outside special-occasion / business dining. Tea pairing is an acquired-taste add-on — for diners who only want wine pairing, the value proposition shrinks.
