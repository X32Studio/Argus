# Xin Rong Ji (新荣记) — Nanjing Xi Lu

## What this work actually does
Shanghai branch of the national Taizhou seafood chain — Beijing flagship holds three Michelin stars, Shanghai branches hold one. Premium banquet positioning.

## Technical mechanism
Wild-caught Taizhou seafood at top end of pricing (1000-2000¥/person typical). Private-rooms-only at this branch. Signature 家烧黄鱼, 沙蒜豆面, 海捕大虾, 东海野生鱼.

## Why it matters for the topic's stated goals
Highest-tier business-dinner option in Shanghai when Taian Table is too foreign and Bao Li Xuan / generic Cantonese fine-dining feels too generic. Brand prestige carries weight with Chinese business guests.

## What is reusable
Multi-city brand template — same chef DNA replicated across Beijing / Shanghai / HK / etc. The brand IS the recommendation.

## What is not safely transferable
Price ceiling is real. Not for casual. The brand recognition matters more than any specific dish — if guest doesn't know the brand, much of the value is lost.

## Evidence quality
High — sustained Michelin retention, national brand, sibling-restaurant comparison data abundant.

## Concrete next experiments or hypotheses
Compare Nanjing Xi Lu branch vs. Yong Fu (Hongkou) — both Taizhou/Ningbo-region seafood; which one is the local-expert pick? Find a 公众号 review that decides between them.
