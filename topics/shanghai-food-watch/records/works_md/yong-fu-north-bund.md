# 甬府 (北外滩来福士店) Yong Fu — North Bund / Raffles Beibund

## 1. What this work actually does
Ningbo cuisine flagship occupying the top three floors of Raffles City Beibund. Promoted to Black Pearl 三钻 in 2026 — alongside 菁禧荟 and 遇外滩 (one of Shanghai's three 三钻 entries).

## 2. Technical mechanism
Weng Yongjun's deliberate "out-lux the hotel Chinese restaurants" brief: premium international ingredients (dried abalone, elephant trunk clams, rockfish), Western kitchen workflows applied to Ningbo backbone. Signature reinterpretation: abalone paired with Ningbo fermented bean sauce (rather than truffle), called out by "abalone prince" Ma Guangfan.

## 3. Why it matters for the topic's stated goals
Most institutionally validated Ningbo / 甬帮 fine dining in Shanghai. Provides a same_group comparison point to the existing `yong-fu-hongkou` shallow entry — these are NOT the same restaurant; one is the original neighborhood branch, this is the 2024+ tower flagship.
