# Fabula (depth-upgrade, iter 2: shallow → medium)

## 1. What this work actually does
Three-chef collaborative tasting-menu kitchen in 静安, opened 2025, awarded Michelin one star on first listing (2026 guide). Located in the attic of a wine bar; the wine bar itself is the casual sibling **Alea** (Zee Zheng's Ningbo-inspired bistro on the ground floor).

## 2. Technical mechanism
- Three chefs jointly drive the kitchen: **Zee Zheng** (former Taian Table sous chef — the established lineage anchor), **Charles Tan**, and **Ricky**.
- East-meets-West tasting menu blending Cantonese precision, Ningbo seafood vocabulary, and Western technique.
- Theatrical presentation flair noted by Michelin inspectors.
- Vertical format: Fabula (tasting menu, attic) above Alea (casual bistro, ground floor) — a deliberate two-tier offering from the same chef.

## 3. Why it matters for the topic's stated goals
- Highest-novelty 2025 opening with institutional validation already in place — Michelin one-star on first listing is the rare case where authority and recency align.
- Multi-chef collaborative format is uncommon in Chinese fine dining; if it sustains, this is a templatable model.
- Lineage edge Taian Table → Fabula → Alea is the canonical chef-lineage trace for the topic.

## 4. What is reusable
