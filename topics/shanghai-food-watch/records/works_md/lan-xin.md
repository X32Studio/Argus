# Lan Xin (兰心餐厅)

## What this work actually does
The textbook 进贤路 cheap-and-excellent neighborhood 本帮 restaurant — Michelin Bib Gourmand for ~9 consecutive years through 2026.

## Technical mechanism
Tiny room, no reservations, no English menu. Classical 本帮 menu — 红烧肉, 炒鳝糊, 油爆虾, 腌笃鲜. Lunch is the easier window.

## Why it matters for the topic's stated goals
The reference "100-180¥/person Shanghainese" recommendation. Different price tier and use case from Old Jesse — solo lunch / walk-in, not destination dinner.

## What is reusable
Pattern of Bib Gourmand longevity as a stronger signal than star longevity for "neighborhood food worth knowing about."

## What is not safely transferable
Small room means it doesn't scale to 4+ guests or to dinner-time reservations. Not for special occasion or atmosphere.

## Evidence quality
High — long Michelin Bib Gourmand retention + 进贤路 strip being well-documented.

## Concrete next experiments or hypotheses
Identify the rest of the 进贤路 strip — what's the cluster around Lan Xin? Build out 进贤路 as a neighborhood mini-cluster.
