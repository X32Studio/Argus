# Nora's Wine Shop & Bar

## 1. What this work actually does
Compact natural-wine bar + bottle shop on the quiet end of 永福路 (Yongfu Lu), 徐汇. Opened April 2025 by proprietor Nora Liu.

## 2. Technical mechanism
Curated natural-leaning wine list (Alsace Pinot Gris, occasional Radikon / Fanny Sabre), rotating selections, small-plate food (burrata gelato, ham-and-cheese, nuts). Retail nook lets diners buy the bottle they tried. Vinyl & Vino nights every other Thursday.

## 3. Why it matters for the topic's stated goals
Anchors a `noras` representative for the 2025 mid-tier neighborhood opening cohort that iter 1's research_state explicitly flagged as missing. Sits in the 武康-永福 cluster (徐汇) that is one of the topic's named priority neighborhoods.
