# 老正兴菜馆 (福州路店) Lao Zheng Xing — Fuzhou Road (depth-upgrade iter 2: shallow → medium)

## 1. What this work actually does
Founded 1862, relocated to 福州路 556号 in 1997. Michelin one-star since 2020 (kept in 2026). Serves classical 本帮菜 to a mixed loyal-local + Michelin-driven tourist crowd.

## 2. Technical mechanism
Old-style 国营 / 老字号 dining-hall format: large round-table halls, traditional Shanghainese signatures (草头圈子, 油爆河虾, 酱方, 糖醋小排), no tasting menu, no design-forward room. The Michelin star reads as 'recognition of tradition' rather than 'recognition of refinement.'
