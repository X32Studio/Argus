# 中国菜·头灶 (Tou Zao)

## What this work actually does
中国菜·头灶 is a Shanghai 中餐 Omakase (Chinese cuisine, counter-seating board-front format) restaurant that opened Jan 2022 in the former 佐々 Japanese omakase space. Black Pearl 一钻 → 二钻 升钻 in 2026, Michelin one-star. ¥2,000-3,000 per person.

## Technical mechanism
- **Format inheritance**: deliberately kept the Japanese-omakase physical setup (counter seating, chef-plated courses, ceremony) but swapped cuisine to Chinese.
- **Pricing tier**: ¥2,000-3,000/person puts it at the top of the Shanghai Chinese fine-dining ladder, alongside Xin Rong Ji Nanjing Xi and Yong Fu North Bund.
- **Operational policies**: reportedly enforces no phone recording, 10+ age minimum — controls the dining environment as a deliberate signal of seriousness.

## Why it matters for the topic's stated goals
- Anchor for the **Black Pearl 升钻 cohort** — one of 5 Shanghai 一钻→二钻 promotions in 2026. Promotion (rather than maintenance) is a stronger institutional signal than first-time listing.
- First worked example of the 中餐 Omakase category — tests whether the format generalizes beyond Japanese cuisine.

## What is reusable
- Format-inheritance pattern: when an existing high-end space closes or pivots, the operational infrastructure (reservation system, counter layout, chef-front-of-house ceremony) is transferable to a different cuisine.
- "Special-occasion-only positioning" is a coherent business model — there's no casual case, and reservations weeks-ahead with per-person ¥2K+ filters self-selecting diners.

## What is not safely transferable
- 中餐 Omakase as a category is contested — Chinese-internet pushback ('中华大冤种' / "Chinese-cuisine sucker tax") suggests the price-vs-cooking ratio is the unresolved question.
- Recording bans and age minima only work if reservation pressure is high enough that the venue can dictate terms. Lower-demand venues would lose customers.

## Evidence quality
Multiple sources confirming Black Pearl 2026 二钻 升钻 status and Michelin one-star (2025 first listed); pricing range corroborated across multiple Chinese-internet reviews. Specific chef name and exact street address not surfaced in this iteration — flag for shallow→medium upgrade work.
