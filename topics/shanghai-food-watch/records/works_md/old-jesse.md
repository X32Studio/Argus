# Old Jesse (老吉士)

## What this work actually does
The cult-favorite small Shanghainese restaurant on 天平路 — possibly the single most-recommended "real" 本帮 spot for visiting foodies since the early 2000s.

## Technical mechanism
Tiny two-floor space, no-frills service, classical 本帮 menu. Signature 红烧肉, 葱烧鱼头, 蟹粉砂锅饭. Reservations are notoriously hard.

## Why it matters for the topic's stated goals
The reference point for "real Shanghai food in a real Shanghai room." Captures the cuisine + experience-design contradiction beautifully — the cramped chaos is the point, but also the disqualifier for some occasions.

## What is reusable
Pattern for surfacing "credible local institution" — long operating history (2004+) + 公众号 coverage + crowd consensus all aligned, despite no Michelin or Black Pearl listing.

## What is not safely transferable
Not for date nights where atmosphere matters. Not for the noise-averse. Not for big groups (room is tiny).

## Evidence quality
Medium-high. Strong editorial consensus and crowd consensus, but lacks Michelin authority and a fresh personal-verdict. Last 公众号 coverage spike was ~2022-2023.

## Concrete next experiments or hypotheses
Cross-check against newer Shanghainese (e.g. Sheng Yong Xing 晟永兴, Ren He Guan 人和馆) to see if Old Jesse is being displaced as the consensus pick, or just remains the default.
